Mediterranean Sea Ancient Pharos http://www.unesco.org/csi/pub/source/alex6.htm This site showcases the French underwater investigations in the sunken harbour of Pharos off the coast of Alexandria in Egypt. Western Indian Ocean Red Sea (Egypt, Israel, Jordan, Sudan, Saudi Arabia, Eritrea, Djibouti, Yemen) Nabataean City of Tayma http://www.dainst.org/index_3258_en.html Archaeological research by the Saudi Commission of Tourism and Antiquities and the German Archaeological Institute, Berlin at the oasis city of Tayma, northwestern part of Saudi Arabia. Tayma was active trading outpost along the ancient incense routes from south Arabia to the Mediterranean around the 8th century BC. The German mission has uncovered a fortress and occupation from early second millennium BC. The main period in the 1st millennium BC, marked by the Thamudic script and the evidence for a Babylonian seat of power. The site is well illustrated with photographs and maps.

Heritage does have its physical manifestations, like the exquisite architecture of the Taj Mahal or the virgin forests of the Amazon. At HeritagewithoutFrontiers, we go beyond the tangible to think of heritage as myriad expressions of quality and beauty unfettered by political boundaries. These sensibilities have shaped human psyche for millenia.Recent finds suggest that early man crafted beads as far back as 200000 years before present. Did you know that certain tribes which live in the rain forests of south India hold the secrets of medicinal herbs? Here is an instance of traditional knowledge gained by remote communities over time. We believe that knowledge about heritage and knowledge that springs from heritage is crucial for a good quality of life. Though much of heritage is concerned with the past, its beauty is enjoyed in the present, like walking around old waterfronts or wearing traditional textiles. Heritage also encompasses the natural world, the forests, the threat of global warming, the need to preserve wildlife. Heritage gives us a sense of continuum and stability. HeritagewithoutFrontiers is an open internet resource which brings together the finest ideas, scholarship and happenings in the areas of cultural and natural heritage. HeritagewithoutFrontiers is not merely an information platform. We seek to connect, interact and present knowledge about heritage in a way that is exciting, rational and relevant..
